The Story of Arcania

It started as a late-night thought in a college dorm room. Surrounded by news of data breaches and privacy concerns, a computer science student wondered, "Why is something as personal as our digital keys in the hands of corporations? Why can't we have a vault that even its creators can't open?"

This question sparked an obsession: to build a password manager that was fully open-source, completely client-side, and fundamentally trustworthy. The project was named **Arcania**, inspired by the word 'arcane'‚Äîmeaning understood by few; mysterious or secret. Arcania was built to protect the modern world's most arcane secrets: our passwords.
